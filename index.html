About

Biography

Xueji You is an associate professor and master's supervisor at Shanghai University. His research is centered on the environmental chemistry of emerging contaminants and green, low-energy remediation technologies for polluted soil and groundwater systems.

His work integrates environmental geochemistry, hydrogeology, computer science, environmental microbiology, and environmental materials, combining laboratory-scale mechanistic investigation, numerical simulation, and pilot-scale application experiments.

Research

Research Directions

Emerging Contaminants and Environmental Processes

Iron- and carbon-based materials in the environmental transformation of emerging contaminants, including natural attenuation and advanced oxidation processes.

Reactive Transport Modeling and AI Prediction

Multiscale and multiprocess coupled modeling of pollutant transport, together with AI-based surrogate models for migration and transformation in aquifers.

Low-Energy Control of Soil-Groundwater Pollution

Control strategies based on natural attenuation, advanced oxidation, and field-oriented remediation design for contaminated soil-groundwater systems.
